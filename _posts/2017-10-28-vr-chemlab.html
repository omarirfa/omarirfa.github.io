This was my capstone or senior design project which I worked on during my bachelors. This project had several aims:

  • To provide a safe area for individuals to perform experiments
  • Majors like petroleum engineering are only available in select regions of United Arab Emirates. The main reason for this was due to the lack of accessible laboratory equipment.
  • Ease of use

You can check out the project on Github.
